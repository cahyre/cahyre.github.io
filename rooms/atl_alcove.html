Atlantean Alcove

The Atlantean Alcove is an immersive seaside retreat where undersea tranquility meets upscale comfort. Ocean-deep teal walls are adorned with ethereal murals of coral, drifting fish, and a graceful jellyfish that glows softly in the ambient light. A plush, shell-shaped headboard anchors the room, echoing the textures and curves of the ocean floor. Sunlight pours through a grand arched window, framing an uninterrupted view of rolling waves just steps beyond the glass. Every detail—from the sea-green furnishings to the coral-embroidered pillows—creates the feeling of sleeping within a serene underwater sanctuary.
