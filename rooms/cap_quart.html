Captain's Quarters

The Captain’s Quarters is an upscale seaside retreat blending nautical elegance with modern comfort. Rich navy textiles, warm teak accents, and brushed brass fixtures create an atmosphere reminiscent of a luxury captain’s cabin. A king-size bed with crisp white linens overlooks panoramic ocean views. Subtle maritime décor—like vintage compasses and rope-trimmed frames—adds charm without overwhelming the space. This room offers guests a refined, tranquil escape inspired by the romance of life at sea.
